ALL ABOUT K-ON!!!!

ABOUT HOBBIES SEASONS

K-ON!! is a Japanese anime and manga series created by Kakifly. It follows the lighthearted story of four high school girls who join their schools light music club to save it from being disbanded. Despite initially lacking much musical experience, they form a band called Ho-kago Tea Time (After School Tea Time) and grow closer as friends while practicing, performing, and enjoying everyday school life filled with humor, slice-of-life moments, and lots of tea and sweets.

Characters

Name: Yui Hirasawa

Role: Lead Guitar, Vocals

Ho-kago Tea Time band member main character of k-on, with no prior guitar experience, quickly became talented through practice. Cheerful, airheaded, and clumsy but very passionate about music. Known for her love of sweets and naps.

Name: Mio Akiyama

Role: Bass, Backup Vocals

Member of Ho-kago Tea Time Joined with her childhood friend Ritsu; shy and reserved but dedicated.Intelligent, serious, and easily embarrassed. Loves writing lyrics but gets nervous on stage.

Band member of Ho-Kago Tea Time the Light Music Club to keep it alive; persuaded Mio to join.Ritsu is Energetic, mischievous, and carefree. The mood-maker of the group, often teasing Mio.

joined the Jazz Club, since she preferred the cooler and more sophisticated image of jazz over the “goofy” vibe of the Light Music Club.She often hangs out with Ui and Azusa, forming a trio of close friends.

Name: Ui Hirasawa

Role: Accordion

She is the younger sister of Yui Hirasawa, the lead guitarist and vocalist of the Light Music Club’s band Ho-kago Tea Timeknown for her maturity, kindness, and caretaking personality. Though not an original Light Music Club member, she supports the band in many ways and later becomes an official member after the seniors graduate.

Name: Sawako Yamanako

Role: Guitar

She is a music teacher at Sakuragaoka High School and becomes the faculty advisor for the Light Music Club after being forced by the members.Sawako has a dual personality. As a teacher, she tries to appear proper, kind, and professional, but her real personality is lively, mischievous, and a bit wild.

Name: Nodoka Manabe

Role:School Representative

Nodoka is calm, mature, and responsible. She has a level-headed and caring nature, often looking out for Yui (who can be forgetful and scatterbrained). Despite her serious demeanor, she’s also warm, approachable, and supportive of her friends’ activities.
